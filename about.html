About CollegeNotes

CollegeNotes was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to help students succeed in their academic journey through better note-taking and knowledge management.

Our platform combines the latest in educational technology with intuitive design to create a note-taking experience that enhances learning and retention.

Our Story

CollegeNotes began when a group of graduate students recognized a common problem: despite the digital revolution, effective note-taking systems for college students remained outdated and inefficient.

We set out to build a solution that would not only help students capture information during lectures but would transform how they organize, revise, and retain knowledge throughout their academic careers.

Today, CollegeNotes serves thousands of students across hundreds of institutions, continually evolving based on user feedback and educational research.
